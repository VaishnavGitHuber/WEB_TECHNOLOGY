HTML Tags and Their Descriptions

  • <p> (Paragraph): Defines a paragraph of text. It is used to structure content into readable blocks.
  • <h1> to <h6> (Headings): Defines headings, with <h1> being the largest and <h6> the smallest.
  • <table> (Table): Creates a table to organize data into rows and columns.
  • <a> (Anchor): Defines a hyperlink that links to another page or location.
