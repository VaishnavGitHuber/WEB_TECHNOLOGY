Mohanlal

Mohanlal Viswanathan, widely known as Mohanlal, is an iconic actor in the Indian film industry, especially in Malayalam cinema. With a career spanning over four decades, he has acted in more than 300 films, showcasing his versatility across various genres such as drama, action, and comedy.

Known for his natural acting style and expressive performances, Mohanlal has received numerous awards, including several National Film Awards and Kerala State Film Awards. He is also the recipient of the Padma Bhushan, one of India's highest civilian honors, for his contributions to the arts.
